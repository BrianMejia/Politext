Politext

Main

As a part of the Congressional App Challenge I wanted to provide an accessible way for people to get statistics in some political elections. With this data people can see where their state stands in something like 2016 Presidential Election, or the Senate race.

Usage

The system has a cell phone # that you would text with your inquiry, in which it will respond to you with the appropriate statistics, if there are any. Applicable topics include:

Topic Phone Input
Presidental Race President [State]
Senate Senate [State]
National House Race House
Candidate's Favorable Rating Favorable Rating [Candidate's Last Name]
Obama's Job Approval Job Approval [Sector/State]
